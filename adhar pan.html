Linking Aadhaar and PAN Card Guide

  1. Visit the Official Website: Go to the official website of UIDAI for Aadhaar and the Income Tax Department for PAN card.
  2. Provide Aadhaar Details: Enter your Aadhaar number and other required details on the linking portal.
  3. Provide PAN Details: Enter your PAN card number and other necessary information.
  4. Verify Details: Ensure that the details provided for both Aadhaar and PAN are accurate.
  5. Submit Linking Request: Submit the linking request through the online portal.
  6. Check Status: You can check the status of your Aadhaar-PAN linking request online.

Please note that these steps are a general guideline. Refer to the official UIDAI and Income Tax Department websites for the most recent and accurate information before linking your Aadhaar and PAN cards.
