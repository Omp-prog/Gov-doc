Aadhaar Card Application Guide

  1. Visit the Official Website: Go to the official website of UIDAI, the authorized agency for issuing Aadhaar cards in India.
  2. Fill out the Aadhaar Enrollment Form: Provide accurate personal information such as name, date of birth, address, and contact details.
  3. Provide Biometric Data: Your fingerprints and iris scan will be captured at the Aadhaar enrollment center.
  4. Receive Aadhaar Acknowledgment Slip: After the enrollment, you'll receive an acknowledgment slip containing your enrollment number.
  5. Check Aadhaar Status: Use the enrollment number to check the status of your Aadhaar card online.
  6. Receive Aadhaar Card: Once processed, your Aadhaar card will be sent to your address.

Please note that these steps are a general guideline. Refer to the official UIDAI website for the most recent and accurate information before applying for an Aadhaar card.
